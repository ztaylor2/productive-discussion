About

Mission Statement: The purpose of this platform is to promote constructive intellectual discourse about controversial topics.

The Premise:

In general the internet tends to hinder productive discussion and debate. This does not have to be the case. It is possible to build a platform on the internet that facilitates productive debate in a way that will benefit and enlighten users. It has the potential to provide a clearer means of determining the winner of the debate and potentially the correct path for us to work for as a society.
